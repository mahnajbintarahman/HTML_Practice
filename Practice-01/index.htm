Simple Omleatte Reacipe


An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omleatte combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total:Appoximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables,cooked meats,hearbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch f salt nd pepper until they well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a flutter textuer.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the omlatte Once the butter is mealted and bubbing,pour in eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface .
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs beging to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle ,sprinkle your bchosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fols ans serve: As the omelette continues to cook,cearfully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute,then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
